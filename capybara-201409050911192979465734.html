why wheedu?

Because you should love being involved with the important groups in your life.

Wheedu is a free technology platform designed to help those who want to bring people together to accomplish a shared collective mission and actually enjoy doing it. Borne from frustrations with email, spreadsheets and websites and inspired by the simple idea that there has to be a better way to bring groups together.

Welcome to the Wheedu community! Love being involved again.

Read About Our Beliefs

Group Page Nirvana

One place to talk, share, plan and collaborate. Seriously.

Tired of digging through 20 group emails? Set up a group page on Wheedu. Your Inbox will thank you.

Calendar Enlightenment

Create events in seconds. Share them instantaneously. Sync to personal calendars.

Our digital trifecta will have you loving your calendar again.

Get Them to the Polls

Group suffrage is here. Now everyone can vote. Just create a poll and watch the returns role in.

Ditch the spreadsheet and let Wheedu keep tabs on everyone’s opinion by tabulating results in real-time.

Volunteering Made Easy

Everyone needs help. Sometimes even with how to ask for help.

Get the help you need by creating simple volunteer tasks. It’s the bees knees in virtual virtuosity.

From To Do to Done

You’re awesome. We get that. Awesome people don’t have time for boring lists of things to do.

The DuList is for someone awesome like you. Discover new things. Help where you can. Then get on with life.

Analytics - The More You Know

Ever wonder why the sky is blue? Where babies come from? Who’s actually contributing to your group?

If the answer is yes to one of these questions then Wheedu’s group analytics engine will provide you with the knowledge you need.
